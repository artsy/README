## Practice Information

### Purpose

Provide a forum for ALL engineers (not just platform engineers) to:

- **Disseminate good patterns:** Share best practices and successful patterns within the platform development.
- **Generate actions to improve the platform:** Identify and discuss actionable items to enhance the platform.
- **Bring what they find important:** Provide a platform for individuals to contribute their insights and concerns.
- **Radiate intent of changes in the Platform:** Communicate the purpose and intent behind any changes.
- **Ensure the platform evolves in a coherent direction:** Ensure alignment among engineers and product teams, steering the platform in a cohesive direction.

### Artifacts

- Each practice will produce the following:
  - Practice notes with the main topics: Document key points and decisions made during the practice. These will be saved in the topic card
  - Possible action items discussed: Identify and outline potential actions for improvement (not mandatory an action item for each practice)
  - Information was shared and people aligned: Ensure information is disseminated, and team members are aligned on the discussed topics.
